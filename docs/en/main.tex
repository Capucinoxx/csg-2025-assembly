  \begin{abstract}
    Innovation in programming relies on the ability to transcend the 
    limitations of existing languages. While these languages are 
    powerful in terms of expressiveness, they can constrain developers 
    creativity by imposing rigid structures. This note proposes the 
    creation of an assembly language based on Presburger arithmetic, 
    a mathematical theory that provides a decidable framework for 
    manipulating integers.
  \end{abstract}
